Facts About Animals

Cats

Cats lack the ability to taste sweetness and cannot see the color blue or green, but they have great night vision. They spend around 70% of their lives sleeping, and a house cat can run at speeds of up to 30 mph.

Panda

Pandas are known for their unique black-and-white fur, which helps with camouflage, and their diet, which is almost entirely bamboo. They spend about 12 hours a day eating up to 40 pounds of bamboo, despite being carnivores by classification.
